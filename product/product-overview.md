# Labs To Legends

## Description
We turn rigorous research and data into real-world solutions with ethics at the core. We use AI to solve the world’s toughest challenges—responsibly, at scale.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Explain the mission quickly and credibly
The landing page clearly communicates what Labs To Legends is, why it exists, and how it works in under a minute using crisp copy and strong visuals.

### Problem 2: Make projects easy to evaluate and explore
A projects section showcases current work and links to project pages with the problem, approach, impact, and status.

### Problem 3: Humanize the movement through researchers
Researcher profiles and personal statements/testimonies help visitors connect with the people behind the work.

### Problem 4: Convert interested researchers into applicants
A prominent “Apply to Join” call-to-action leads to a simple form (name + email) for follow-up and next steps.

### Problem 5: Enable collaboration and partner inquiries
A collaboration/partner inquiry form provides a clear path to start working with Labs To Legends.

## Key Features
- Project index + project detail pages
- Researcher profiles + statements/testimonies
- Blog / insights / articles
- Team / about page (story + values)
- Prominent “Apply to Join” CTA with simple name/email form
- Collaboration / partner inquiry form
- Image-forward storytelling across projects and people
