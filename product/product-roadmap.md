# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Home & Narrative
Mission, differentiator, visuals, and primary calls-to-action.

### 2. Projects
Project index plus project detail pages that communicate problem, approach, impact, and status.

### 3. Researchers
Researcher profiles and personal statements/testimonies to humanize the movement.

### 4. Join & Collaborate
Simple apply flow (name/email) and a partner inquiry path for collaboration.

### 5. Insights
Blog/articles to share updates, ideas, and progress.
